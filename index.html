Scribal Institute (Pty) Ltd is a quantitative advisory firm headquartered in Gaborone, Botswana. We build investment and economic frameworks on mathematical and statistical rigour, then deliver them in a live advisory and analytical context — translating research into decisions that clients can act on, monitor, and hold us accountable to.

The firm is structured around four integrated divisions: Financial Services, Economics Analysis, Financial Education, and Fintech Product Development. Each division reinforces the others — research sharpened in advisory work feeds the classroom, and methods proven across all three become the basis for the products we build.

MISSION

To make rigorous, evidence-led quantitative analysis accessible to investors, institutions, governments, and learners — and to set the standard for quantitative finance and economic practice in Botswana and the wider region.

VISION

A regional financial and economic ecosystem in which decisions are framework-driven and evidence-led, supported by a generation of quantitatively fluent professionals trained to the highest international standards.

HOW WE THINK

Four principles guide every engagement.

i.

Evidence over intuition

Recommendations are derived from data, tested models, and documented assumptions — never from narrative alone. Every finding is traceable to its inputs and reproducible.

ii.

Risk first, return second

We size and stress every strategy and forecast against its uncertainty before we ask what it can earn. Risk-adjusted and confidence-bounded results are the metrics that matter.

iii.

Transparent frameworks

Clients see the logic, not just the conclusion. Every model and framework we deliver is auditable and reproducible. Opacity erodes trust.

iv.

Rigour that travels

We hold ourselves to international quantitative standards, so the methods we use in Gaborone would stand up in London, Singapore, Washington or Brussels.
