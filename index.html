Who We Are

African in context.
Global in standard.

"Decisions improve when they are grounded in explicit method rather than intuition, and when uncertainty is measured before conclusions are drawn."

Headquartered in Gaborone, Botswana. Operating to the highest international standards in quantitative finance and economic modelling.

Scribal Institute (Pty) Ltd is a quantitative advisory firm headquartered in Gaborone, Botswana. We build investment and economic frameworks on mathematical and statistical rigour, then deliver them in a live advisory and analytical context — translating research into decisions that clients can act on, monitor, and hold us accountable to.

The firm is structured around four integrated divisions: Financial Services, Economics Analysis, Financial Education, and Fintech Product Development. Each division reinforces the others — research sharpened in advisory and analytical work feeds the classroom, and methods proven across all three become the basis for the products we build.

A rare combined competence: quantitative finance and applied economic modelling under one roof. Our mandate is to bring institutional-grade quantitative method to a market that has long been underserved by it — without diluting the rigour that defines it.

Mission

To make rigorous, evidence-led quantitative analysis accessible to investors, institutions, governments, and learners — and to set the standard for quantitative finance and economic practice in Botswana and the wider region.

Vision

A regional financial and economic ecosystem in which decisions are framework-driven and evidence-led, supported by a generation of quantitatively fluent professionals trained to the highest international standards.

How We Think

Four principles guide every
engagement, analysis, and product.

i.

Evidence over intuition

Recommendations are derived from data, tested models, and documented assumptions — never from narrative alone. Every finding should be traceable to its inputs and reproducible by any qualified reviewer.

ii.

Risk first, return second

We size and stress every strategy and forecast against its uncertainty before we ask what it can earn or imply. Risk-adjusted and confidence-bounded results are the metrics that matter — not raw return or point estimates.

iii.

Transparent frameworks

Clients see the logic, not just the conclusion. Every model and framework we deliver is auditable and reproducible. Opacity erodes trust — in financial advice and in economic analysis alike.

iv.

Rigour that travels

We hold ourselves to international quantitative standards, so the methods we use in Gaborone would stand up in any market or ministry — London, Singapore, Washington, or Brussels.
